Check Your Water Consumption and how you can reduce it.

Steps:

  1. Enter all your daily water usage.
  2. Add your daily water consumption using our calculator.
  3. If your water usage at home is exceeding 50 litres per day at your home. Implement these steps:
    • Thrift and mend your clothes
    • Install low-flow fixtures
    • Aerate your lawn
